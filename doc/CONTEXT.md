# What is my plan?
This project begins by cleaning and restructuring the original AP Research dataset into an analysis-ready format. Using SQL, I compute descriptive statistics and analyze differences in perceived stress levels between AP STEM and Humanities students.

To extend the scope of the study, I will integrate external datasets on student stress to compare AP students with broader populations. This addresses the limitation of the original research being restricted to a single high school.

Building on these combined datasets, I will explore patterns associated with elevated stress levels and use them to develop a predictive model for identifying students at risk of academic fatigue or burnout. The goal is to move beyond descriptive analysis and demonstrate how student stress data can be used for early detection and intervention.

This project transitions from a static research study into an applied data analysis framework with potential for real-world impact in academic support systems.
